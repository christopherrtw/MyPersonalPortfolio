Music

For me, music is my constant companion and creative outlet. I have a very diverse taste that ranges from R&B to Rock. However, I enjoy listening to SA/UK underground the most. This includes artists such as Jaykatana, K1llbrady, YT, Fimiguerrero and Babylowsk1 among others.

I'm fascinated by the technical aspects of music production and how technology has transformed the way we create and consume music. This interest complements my Information Systems studies by giving me insights into digital media systems and user experience design.
